2. Interior_Influences

Interior Influences is an interior design company based in Central London, which specialises in working with luxury brands. The final Monogram contains both a lowercase 'i' and an uppercase 'I'. The uppercase can be seen vertically and the lowercase horizontally. This results in a versatile logo that can be used in any situation, colour and position. The last two images consist of rug designs displayed and sold at the Ideal Home Show 2015.

3. Equigienist

Equigienist is a veterinary biopharma startup based in the UK, who produce and distribute dental chews intended to improve oral hygiene in horses. The client requested the logo to appear educational/clinical and was attracted to a minimalistic design. The 'E' shaped logogram is an artistic representation of an acidic molecule morphing into an alkaline. Following from this, green was chosen for the primary colour of the design, as green indicates a neutral pH.
